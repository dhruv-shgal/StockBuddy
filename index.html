Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is StockBuddy? +
Our ensemble model achieves 91.2% directional accuracy on backtested data. However, past performance doesn't guarantee future results. We provide confidence intervals with every prediction to help you understand uncertainty.
Is the data live? +
Yes, StockBuddy pulls real-time market data from Yahoo Finance API. Predictions are updated continuously as new market data becomes available.
Can I integrate it into my trading system? +
Absolutely. StockBuddy offers a RESTful API that you can integrate into your trading systems. Check our technical documentation for API endpoints and authentication details.
Can I add my own models? +
Yes, StockBuddy is open-source and modular. You can train custom models and integrate them into the prediction ensemble. See our GitHub repository for contribution guidelines.
